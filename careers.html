Why Huge?

What would the world be like if companies put user needs first?

Huge was founded in an attempt to answer this question. Two decades later we've demonstrated that industries change and lives are improved when design, technology, and communications come together to solve problems for people. To do this, we've built a company of experts with diverse backgrounds who believe technology should be used to deliver better services and experiences for people. We give them the freedom to make things that matter, working alongside colleagues they respect.

We're a company that prizes entrepreneurialism and embraces constant change. Over the years, five perspectives have come to define the way we work:
